The Palm Theatre is one of the gems of SLO that is keeping me here. Their movie showing process is archaic and unique, and I love it that way: a selection of monthly pre-ordained moves fitting certain themes, genres, or filmographies. It is always such a joy go through and add their showings to my calendar. Thank you for being a safe space; one that I can always come to and cry my sorrows out and laugh harder than I ever have.
